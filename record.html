The UNBWR

The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR), USA documents and publishes exceptional, unbreakable records along with associated concepts, prototypes, items, and official merchandise such as branded T-shirts. These artifacts serve as lasting symbols of achievement and inspiration for future generations.

what we do

The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR), USA is dedicated to publishing, promoting, and distributing its official world records books in both print and digital (e-book) formats. In addition to its primary record-related publications, UNBWR is also committed to curating and releasing books across a diverse range of subjects, including but not limited to: religion, spirituality, health and wellness, science and technology, personal development, environmental awareness, and socio-economic transformation.

why us

The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR), USA is a globally recognized and authorized organization specializing in the issuance of world record certifications. Operating transcontinentally from the United States, it functions through a broad international network to reach individuals and communities worldwide.UNBWR is dedicated to recognizing and certifying exceptional human potential — including talent, expertise, brilliance, creativity, skill, and innovation — through its universal certification standards. The organization also honors distinguished individuals and acknowledges remarkable places that have made significant contributions to humanity, global progress, and universal peace.

Process and Procedure

Below are the simplified steps for the application process and procedure:

  1. Fill the online form given on the website.
  2. Immediate reply from UNBWR Organization.
  3. Read Accept Terms and Conditions and confirm via form.
  4. Review committee will issue report.
  5. Declaration of record from UNBWR USA.

Record Policies

  • Originality Requirement
    Proposed record titles must be distinctive, original, and not duplicate any existing record.
  • Accurate Information
    All required details must be completed truthfully and with full accuracy. Incomplete or misleading information may result in disqualification.
  • Mandatory Evidence
    Submission of valid photo and/or video documentation is compulsory for verification and approval of any record attempt.
  • Processing Timeline
    The standard processing and certification period is up to 30 days from the date of complete submission.
  • Authority of UNBWR
    The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR) reserves the exclusive right to approve or decline any application based on internal assessment and verification outcomes.

Record Consultancy

If you are interested in applying for a new record title or seeking professional guidance, please complete the official application form. Once your application is submitted, our team will review the details and respond within 15 business days with the next steps in the process.

Distinction Pursuit

To set or break a record under the United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR), USA, applicants are encouraged to carefully review previously established records. If you wish to challenge an existing record, you must submit your claim within 30 days of its official declaration.

Data Confidentiality

The issued certificate is an exclusive document and must not be duplicated or redistributed without the express, written authorization of the Board.
