The UNBWR

The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR), USA stores and publishes the unbreakable records with ideas, model, item, and T-shirt.

Process and Procedure

Below are the simplified steps for the application process and procedure:

  1. Fill the online form given on the website.
  2. Immediate reply from UNBWR Organization.
  3. Read Accept Terms and Conditions and confirm via form.
  4. Review committee will issue report.
  5. Declaration of record from UNBWR USA.

Record Policies

  • Record title should be unique.
  • Details must be filled accurately.
  • Video/photo proof is mandatory.
  • Process requires 30 days for certificate.
  • UNBWR has rights to accept or reject based on verification.

Record Consultancy

If you want to apply for a new record title and you seek consultancy, kindly fill the application form. The fee is non-refundable once submitted.

Legal Points

The awarded certificate should not be reproduced without prior permission. All disputes are subject to the jurisdiction of California, USA.
