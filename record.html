The UNBWR

The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR) honors the extraordinary by documenting and publishing unbreakable achievements, along with their visionary concepts, prototypes, and creations. Through our exclusive official merchandise, such as branded T-shirts, we transform milestones into symbols of legacy — preserving the spirit of excellence for generations to come. At UNBWR, every record tells a story, and every artifact becomes a timeless inspiration for the world to witness, remember, and aspire to.

what we do

(UNBWR) is committed to the publishing, promotion, and global distribution of its official world record books in both print and digital formats. Beyond celebrating world records, UNBWR also curates and releases publications across a diverse spectrum of subjects — including religion, spirituality, health and wellness, science and technology, personal growth, environmental consciousness, and socio-economic transformation. Through these works, UNBWR aims to enrich minds, inspire progress, and contribute meaningfully to the evolution of global thought.

why us

(UNBWR) is an internationally acclaimed and officially empowered institution that specializes in the recognition and certification of extraordinary achievements. With a vast global presence, UNBWR connects with diverse cultures and communities to spotlight outstanding milestones across the world. UNBWR is devoted to honoring exceptional human capabilities — including talent, mastery, ingenuity, originality, expertise, and innovation — through its globally respected validation framework. In addition to record certification, the organization pays tribute to influential individuals and landmark places that have contributed profoundly to human advancement, societal transformation, and global harmony.

Process and Procedure

Follow these simplified steps to apply for a world record with UNBWR:

  1. Submit the Online Application Form-Complete the official form available on our website.
  2. Receive an Immediate Acknowledgement-You will receive a prompt response from the UNBWR team.
  3. Review & Accept Terms and Conditions-Carefully read the terms and confirm your agreement through the provided form.
  4. Evaluation by the Review Committee-Our expert panel will assess your application and issue a detailed report.
  5. Official Record Declaration-Upon approval, your achievement will be formally recognized and declared by UNBWR.

Record Policies

  • Originality Requirement
    Proposed record titles must be distinctive, original, and not duplicate any existing record.
  • Accurate Information
    All required details must be completed truthfully and with full accuracy. Incomplete or misleading information may result in disqualification.
  • Mandatory Evidence
    Submission of valid photo and/or video documentation is compulsory for verification and approval of any record attempt.
  • Processing Timeline
    The standard processing and certification period is up to 30 days from the date of complete submission.
  • Authority of UNBWR
    The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR) reserves the exclusive right to approve or decline any application based on internal assessment and verification outcomes.

Record Consultancy

For aspirants seeking to propose an original world record or to navigate the process with professional insight, we invite you to complete our official application form. Upon receipt, your submission will undergo a meticulous review by our expert panel. Within 15 business days, we will provide personalized guidance outlining the recommended steps to advance your record journey with UNBWR.

Distinction Pursuit

To set or redefine a world record under the banner of the United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR), individuals are encouraged to first study the standards of existing achievements.Should you wish to challenge an established record, your intent must be formally registered within 30 days of the record’s declaration. This ensures fairness, transparency, and adherence to our international protocols.

Data Confidentiality
