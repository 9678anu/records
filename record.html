The UNBWR

At the United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR), we don’t just certify achievements — we immortalize them. From revolutionary ideas to physical feats, each record is a story of vision, grit, and triumph. Through exclusive merchandise like our signature T-shirts and memorabilia, we transform milestones into living legacies — symbols of inspiration for future generations.

what we do

UNBWR is more than a record-keeping body — it's a global platform for publication, promotion, and purpose-driven storytelling. We publish official record books both in print and digital formats, but our work goes far beyond that. Our editorial team curates groundbreaking publications across diverse themes — from spirituality and health to science, innovation, sustainability, and human development. Every page we print is designed to inform, inspire, and influence global consciousness.

why us

We are an internationally acclaimed institution with the authority to validate greatness across borders. Our reach extends to cultures, communities, and change-makers worldwide. Whether it’s talent, innovation, artistry, or impact — UNBWR offers a trusted, prestigious platform for recognition. We don’t just honor records — we celebrate people and places that are shaping the future of humanity.

Your Journey to Recognition: Simple Steps

  1. Apply Online – Complete the official application form on our website.
  2. Immediate Response – Receive a confirmation message from our team.
  3. Review and Agreement – Accept our terms & conditions through a digital form.
  4. Expert Evaluation – Your entry is reviewed by our senior panel.
  5. Official Declaration – Upon approval, your record is officially certified and published.

Policies for Record Validation

  • Originality Every record must be completely unique — we don’t repeat history, we redefine it.
  • Accuracy All entries must be truthful, transparent, and thoroughly completed.
  • Mandatory Proof Photo and video evidence is required for validation — authenticity is non-negotiable.
  • Processing Time Standard review and certification take up to 30 business days.
  • Final Authority UNBWR holds exclusive rights to approve, reject, or revoke records based on comprehensive assessment.

Record Consultancy & Guidance

Not sure where to begin? Our expert consultancy service helps you design and propose a meaningful, record-worthy submission. After reviewing your application, we’ll provide tailored advice within 15 working days to fast-track your achievement journey.

Challenge the Best – Distinction Pursuit

If you're aiming to break an existing record, you must submit your challenge within 30 days of the original announcement. This ensures fair competition, transparency, and adherence to international standards.

Confidentiality & Legal Terms
