The United Nation Book of World Records is the Part Unit of “United Nation Global Services” (Registered Organization under the Government of California, The United States of America).

It is accredited by the UN IGO and the United Nations Accreditation for International Standard Organizations (UNISO).

It has membership status with the United States of America Presidential Advisory Board, and Presidential Award Forum...
