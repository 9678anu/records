About

Constituted in 2020

The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR) is a dedicated division of United Nation Global Services, a registered organization under the Government of California, United States of America.

UNBWR is accredited by the UN IGO and the United Nations Accreditation for International Standard Organizations (UNISO).

We proudly hold membership status with the United States Presidential Advisory Board and the Presidential Award Forum.

In addition to world record certification, we provide prestigious platforms for Awards, Talent Recognition, Corporate Honors, and more.

purpose of unbwr

  • our purpose is to ignite opportunities by recognizing and celebrating extraordinary achievements across the globe.
  • We empower individuals to set and break world records, helping them realize their true potential.
  • Our mission goes beyond recognition — we inspire lives, motivate people, and entertain the world through our one-of-a-kind platform.
  • UNBWR also serves as a prestigious stage for honoring excellence through Business Awards, Talent Awards, Fashion Awards, and more — shining a light on those who strive for greatness in every field.

Mission Of UNBWR

  • To provide a motivational platform for individuals to set and break records.
  • We aim to nurture innovative talent, encourage creativity, and promote thought leadership through record-breaking achievements.

Vision Of UNBWR

  • To inspire the world by connecting people through genuine talent and excellence.
  • To become a globally recognized Book of Records, celebrated for authenticity and trust.
  • We strive to recognize and honor motivation, inspiration, and achievement across all walks of life.

Values Of UNBWR

  • Honesty, Gratitude, Influence, and Empathy
  • Integrity and Trust
  • Respect and Dignity for Every Applicant
