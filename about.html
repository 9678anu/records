About

• About < Home

Founded in 2020 – Rooted in Excellence

The United Nation Book of World Records (UNBWR) was established as a proud initiative under United Nation Global Services,
officially registered under the Government of California, USA. More than just an organization, UNBWR is a global benchmark of recognition,
accredited by the UN IGO and the United Nations Accreditation for International Standard Organizations (UNISO) —
standing as a beacon of authenticity and international credibility.

Global Presence, Presidential Trust

  • We exist to uncover brilliance and open doors for dreamers worldwide.
  • At UNBWR, we don’t just count records — we celebrate ambition, creativity, and courage.
  • Our goal is simple yet powerful: to spark global transformation by honoring human potential in its most extraordinary form.

Mission Of UNBWR

  • To build a platform where dreams are validated, efforts are immortalized, and excellence is rewarded.
  • We champion originality, nurture risk-takers, and highlight changemakers through verified achievements.
  • From fashion to finance, from performance to innovation — we are the voice of limitless aspiration.

Vision Of UNBWR

  • To become the world’s most trusted destination for recognizing real talent, genuine passion, and unshakable determination.
  • We dream of a world where every outstanding feat — big or small — receives the spotlight it deserves.

Values Of UNBWR

  • Truth Above All – Every record verified with fairness and transparency.
  • Gratitude in Action – We honor every journey with humility and respect.
  • Impact That Lasts – From local heroes to global icons, every story matters.
  • Human First – Integrity, empathy, and dignity guide every decision we make.
